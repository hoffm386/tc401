Relationship Initiation

Relationship initiation has three phases:

  1. Awareness
    1. In a Facebook friendship, this stage can happen before the two people meet in person
      1. Friend suggestions use an algorithm to find friends
      2. Stories in News Feed can introduce users to friends of friends
  2. Superficial/Surface Contact
  3. Communication of a Connection

Sprecher, S. & Guynn, L. (2009). Initiation of Relationships. Encyclopedia of Human Relationships.
